## Why Choose a Franchise Model?

Starting an EV charging business from scratch requires expertise in hardware, software, installation, maintenance, and operations. A franchise model provides a proven framework that reduces risk and accelerates time-to-revenue.

## Investment Tiers

### Tier 1: Basic Station (INR 15-25 Lakhs)
- 2 AC chargers (7.4 kW each)
- 1 DC fast charger (30 kW)
- Basic canopy and signage
- CPMS software license
- Suitable for: Parking lots, small commercial spaces

### Tier 2: Standard Station (INR 25-40 Lakhs)
- 4 AC chargers (7.4-22 kW)
- 1-2 DC fast chargers (60 kW)
- Full canopy, lighting, and branding
- CPMS + customer mobile app
- Suitable for: Highway locations, malls, office parks

### Tier 3: Premium Hub (INR 40-75 Lakhs)
- 4-6 AC chargers (22 kW)
- 2-4 DC fast chargers (60-120 kW)
- Customer lounge, retail space
- Full CPMS suite with fleet management
- Suitable for: Highway corridors, large commercial complexes

## ROI Calculations

### Revenue Streams
1. **Charging fees:** INR 12-18 per kWh (margin: INR 3-6/kWh)
2. **Parking fees:** Additional revenue where applicable
3. **Advertising:** Digital displays at charging stations
4. **Ancillary services:** Cafe, convenience store

### Typical Payback Period
- High-traffic location: 2-3 years
- Medium-traffic location: 3-4 years
- Low-traffic location: 4-5 years

## Timeline: From Signing to Revenue

1. **Month 1:** Site assessment and agreement
2. **Month 2:** Electrical infrastructure and civil work
3. **Month 3:** Charger installation and commissioning
4. **Month 4:** Software setup, testing, and launch
